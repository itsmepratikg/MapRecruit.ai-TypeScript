# MapRecruit AI Google Workspace Integration Guide

## Overview
This guide covers how to securely link your Google Workspace account (Gmail, Calendar, Drive, Google Chat) directly into your MapRecruit Workspace.

1. Navigate to Settings > Workspace Configurations.
2. Under the "Integrations" tab, select Google.
3. Click "Connect Account" and follow the standard OAuth flow.
4. If integrating Google Chat specifically, type `@MapRecruitBot` in the specific space you want to link.

## Supported Features:
- Sync Google Calendar Events
- Attach resumes directly from Google Drive
- Receive AI Notifications directly via Google Chat Magic Links

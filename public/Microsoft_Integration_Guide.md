# MapRecruit AI Microsoft Account Integration Guide

## Overview
This guide covers how to securely link your Microsoft account (Office 365, Teams, Calendar) directly into your MapRecruit Workspace.

1. Navigate to Settings > Workspace Configurations.
2. Under the "Integrations" tab, select Microsoft.
3. Click "Connect Account" and follow the standard OAuth flow.
4. Ensure pop-ups are allowed for `maprecruit.ai` domains.

## Supported Features:
- Sync Outlook Calendar Events
- Enable Teams Co-presence links
- One-click Outlook Mail Drafts
